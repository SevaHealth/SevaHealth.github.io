About Dr. Kaur

Jaspreet Kaur, M.D., FMCP-M, AHC

Dr. Jaspreet Kaur is a board-certified Internal Medicine physician with over 25 years of experience caring for patients across a wide range of medical settings. She earned her medical degree from Maulana Azad Medical College in New Delhi, India, and completed her internship and residency at Westchester County Medical Center, Valhalla, New York, an affiliate of New York Medical College.

Throughout her career, Dr. Kaur has been guided by a deep commitment to listening to her patients and understanding the full picture of their health. She has seen firsthand how many individuals with chronic conditions are left searching for answers, often feeling unheard or frustrated by ongoing symptoms.

This experience led her to expand her training in Functional Medicine through the Institute for Functional Medicine, where she focuses on identifying and addressing the root causes of illness. She also pursued her long-standing interest in Ayurveda and is a certified Ayurvedic Health Counselor through the National Ayurvedic Medical Association, allowing her to integrate time-honored healing principles with modern medical care.

Dr. Kaur has practiced in hospital, outpatient, and academic environments, including serving on the faculty at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences. She has also served as a board member for Mission Health Network at Mission Regional Medical Center, where she contributed to physician credentialing and assisted in negotiating service agreements with health insurance companies on behalf of physicians.

As the founder of Seva Health, she provides a more personalized and thoughtful approach to care — one that prioritizes time, attention, and partnership. She works closely with patients facing autoimmune diseases, cancer, especially breast cancer, and complex chronic conditions, helping them better understand their health and explore meaningful, sustainable paths to healing.

Dr. Kaur's goal is simple: to ensure every patient feels heard, supported, and guided with clarity and compassion throughout their journey to better health.

Ayurveda

An Ancient Science of Balance & Daily Living

Ayurveda, meaning "Science of Life," is one of the world's oldest systems of holistic medicine. It teaches that health is achieved by living in harmony with both our body's natural rhythms and the rhythms of nature.

Simple daily habits such as consistent mealtimes, appropriate exercise, and restful sleep, when aligned with our internal biological rhythms, can help prevent illness and maintain overall well-being. The modern-day sciences of chronobiology and chrononutrition also state the importance of these routines. Similarly, adjusting our routines to reflect seasonal changes supports the body's natural ability to rebuild, rejuvenate, and adapt.

Ayurveda is based on the understanding that five fundamental elements (space, air, fire, water, and earth) exist both in the universe and within the human body. The balance and interaction of these elements determine our state of health, while imbalances may lead to illness or disease.

During an Ayurvedic consultation, your unique constitution is assessed to provide personalized recommendations. These may include guidance on diet, daily routines, herbs and spices, oils, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices. This holistic system emphasizes the deep connection between mind, body, and spirit.

Ayurveda believes that “The true healer lies within.”

It teaches you how to unlock your body's innate wisdom to heal by supporting it through simple lifestyle practices. It helps you to repair, rejuvenate, rebuild and restore your health.
